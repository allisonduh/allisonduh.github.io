# Zettelkasten + Obsidian

The idea of Zettelkasten has been very appealing to me for years, but my efforts at implementing were often directionless and overly reliant on the structures of others' examples. I used [Bob Doto's book, a System for Writing](https://bobdoto.computer) as a manual and guide, and found it incredibly helpful in finally understanding the rhizomatic idea structure of the Zettelkasten. 

As a method for implementing a form of Zettelkasten, Obsidian's UI and flexibility are perfect and delightful.
