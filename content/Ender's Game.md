Andrew Yu told me in 5th grade that this was a good book, but it had some bad words in it. Sold.

It was my all-time favorite book in my childhood. As I've gotten older, I've enjoyed seeing how my experience of the book has shifted. As a kid, I was in awe of Ender's excellence, his ability to control his enemies. The snippets showing the manipulation of the IF commanders in the beginning of each chapter were a bore. Upon a recent re-reading, the adults responsible for his world and their doubts and challenges of each other were suddenly much more interesting than I remembered.

If you like this book, the sequel, Speaker for the Dead, is possibly even better.
